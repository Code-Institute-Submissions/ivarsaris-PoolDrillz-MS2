About PoolDrillz

I started PoolDrillz as a project for all pool players. In my opinion, there is stucture missing when learning how to play pool. There is no clear path to becoming a better player. With PoolDrillz, I hope to make it easier for pool players of any level to find the right exercises and to create a community of players and trainers. If you have any suggestions, feedback, questions, or would like to collaborate with me, please e-mail me at ivarsaris@gmail.com
